Types Of Online Slots That Can Be Played For Free

When choosing slot machines to play for free, you can select from different categories of games. The free slot machines  in each of the following categories offer something different, with unique features to keep you entertained.

Video Slots

Video slots are action-packed, promising the most excitement when you play these online slots free. Top-quality graphics and audio in these free online slots, coupled with an inherent storyline in each, make these the most popular game.

Many video slots consist of 5 reels with 5 rows and have multiple paylines. Bonus features might include wilds, scatters, or free spins. These features bump up your chances to win even when you play these casino slots online for free. 

Fruit Slots

Fruit slots are a throwback to the early days of the slot machine. The basic gameplay attracts even the more hardened bettor, drawn to these free slot’ simplicity. 

The fruit symbols in these free slot machines pay out the lowest amount for the most common fruits. The rarest fruit symbols have a higher payout when part of a winning combination.

Progressive Slots

Thanks to technology, progressive slots feature in the slots machine mix at both online and land-based casinos. Through a network of connected slot machines, the jackpot in these games builds up over time. Each time a player places a bet, the jackpot increases by a small percentage.

You can play free slots online with progressive jackpots, but you can’t claim a win unless you're playing with real money. It’s good to practice casino slots with free games before playing for real money and placing your bet.
