Different types of free online Blackjack

This popular card game is available in several variations, the descriptions of which you can find below. Choose and play blackjack free games!

European blackjack

This type is usually played with 2 decks, makes dealers stand on soft 17, and has slightly better odds.

Atlantic city blackjack

Being most popular in Atlantic City, New Jersey, this game has pretty standard rules like doubling down on any 2 cards.

American blackjack 3:2

The difference between the 3:2 and 6:5 versions is that you are paid $3 for every $2 you bet, and the 6:5 offers $6 for every $5.

Vegas strip blackjack

This blackjack online free version is played with 4 decks, dealers stand on soft 17, and Aces can be split once only.
